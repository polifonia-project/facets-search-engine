Document loading

FACETS support the search of music documents in MEI, MusicXML, XML, HUMDRUM and ABC formats. Loading multiple music documents may be done by uploading a ZIP archive. Metadata files may also be included in this ZIP archive. Note that the ZIP archive should not include any folder. It is highly recommended to include title and composer in the music score or metadata file, in order to allow for faceted search navigation.
